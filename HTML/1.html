IBM Watson

     Watson is a question-answering computer system capable of answering questions posed in natural language,developed in IBM's DeepQA. Watson uses IBM's DeepQA software and the Apache UIMA (Unstructured Information Management Architecture) framework. The system was written in various languages, including Java, C++, and Prolog. 

   The Watson capabilities have been extended and the way in which Watson works has been changed to take advantage of new deployment models
(Watson on IBM Cloud) andivolved machine learning capabilities and optimised hardware available to developers and researchers.
